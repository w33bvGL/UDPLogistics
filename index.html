Frequently asked questions

Insurance company covers full or partial amount of the cost for the lost parcel
(it can be a box, luggage bag etc).

If you have chosen to package your parcel yourself then you have to make sure
that you will apply following criteria:

  1. Your parcel must be sealed properly with tape, cling film or boxed and taped.
    It must be stable and should not get ripped while transportation
  2. If you parcel is fragile item and you have chosen to pack it your self then it must
    be covered with bubble wrap and placed in to the crafted or wooden box. Fragile
    tape mast be applied, UPD logistics does not take responsibility of broken item.
  3. Each of your parcel must be covered and labelled with your unique number
    which shown on your declaration form.

Yes, we do offer safe packaging which keeps your fragile item safe and

What is restricted to send?

Please note that all shipments of personal goods MUST NOT contain any of the following
items without being declared to UDP Logistics and pre-arrangements being made to
transport them safely and in accordance with IATA/ICAO dangerous goods regulations.

Lithium Ion Batteries and items containing lithium ion batteries

(e.g. Mobile Phones/Laptop Computers/Cordless power tools)

Flammable liquids

(e.g. perfumes/paint)

Flammable solids

(e.g. matches)

Explosive materials

(e.g. fireworks)

Infectious

substances

Petrol or diesel powered equipment with residue of fuel

(e.g. generators, chainsaws etc.) -Aerosols ( e.g. deodorant sprays)

Flammable gases

(e.g. lighter fuel)

Corrosive Material

(e.g. acid/car batteries)

Magnetise

material

Radioactive

material

It is an offence by international law to ship the above goods without being correctly
packed, marked and declared to the airline and offenders may be subject to heavy fines.
Any shipments containing the above without being declared will not be despatched.

Firearms
Live animals
Pornography
Controlled Drugs
Anything made of endangered species (e.g. Ivory)
Fresh food/meat
